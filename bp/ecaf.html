This blog has some basic concept about Electric charges and field in Physics.

// Electric charge

Just like every body has mass (mass is amount of substance), everybody has electric charge.

SI Unit: Coulomb, C

Sub atomic particles: electron, neutron, proton.

Sub atomic particles are breakable in (anti)quark. (anti)quark -> electron -> atom -> element

Rutherford told, proton is in the atom's nucleus and nucleus is somewhat in the centre of the atom.

Only electron can be imported and exported from the atom because it requires much more energy to manipulate the same with the proton from the nucleus.

// Method of charging

Note: Only applies on insulators because conductors flow the current.

i. By friction

When two body rub on each other, they get charged because of transfer of electron.

ii. Induction

When any charged body placed near the uncharged body without having touched, the uncharged body get oppositely charged.

When any charged body touch the uncharged body, the charge of charged body get transfered to the uncharged body.
